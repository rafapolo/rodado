CPF (individual) and CNPJ (company) tax IDs — read-only access, no PII export endpoints, credential isolation per service.

Corporate Compliance
Beneficial Ownership & Conflict-of-Interest Network
↗ Live demo

Investigators needed to trace beneficial ownership chains and detect undisclosed conflicts of interest across thousands of interconnected companies and individuals — a process that previously required weeks of manual registry lookups across disconnected government sources.

Data model Company (CNPJ) ↔ Person (CPF) via br_me_cnpj.socios — multi-hop graph traversal across ownership layers
Application Interactive force-directed network — nodes are entities, edges are relationships, color-coded by type and role
Ontology objects Company → Person (director) → Company (subsidiary) → PublicContract — same traversal pattern as a Foundry investigation application
Reusability Pattern reused for Senator corporate mapping, Tiger Game ownership network, and Swiss-Brazil entity analysis
→ Outcome: Multi-hop ownership chains surfaced in seconds. Investigators confirmed undisclosed cross-entity links between politically exposed persons and public procurement winners — previously invisible across fragmented registries.

Political Risk & Governance
Senator Corporate Network — Conflict of Interest Mapping

A governance team needed to map the full disclosed and undisclosed corporate network of an elected senator — identifying potential conflicts between their business interests and public budget votes, regulatory decisions, and government contract awards.

Data model TSE candidatos (CPF) → CNPJ socios → CNPJ empresas → CGU contratos — four-entity join across electoral, corporate, and procurement registries
Application Force-directed network with contract value annotations. Nodes sized by total public contract exposure. Edges weighted by ownership share
Ontology depth 3 ownership layers traversed. Linked Person objects (CPF) to Company objects (CNPJ) to PublicContract objects — exactly the Foundry ontology traversal pattern
Transferability Same pattern applies in pharma: clinical investigator (Person) → institution (Company) → trial sponsor contracts (Contract) — conflict-of-interest detection
→ Outcome: 47 companies, 3 holding layers, and R$12M in contracts with entities linked to the senator's corporate network identified — several involving companies where the senator held directorship simultaneously with budget committee membership.

Compliance Company integrity check

A compliance team verifies whether companies awarded public contracts have directors appearing in other sanctioned or flagged entities.

SELECT e.razao_social, e.cnpj, s.nome_socio,
       COUNT(DISTINCT e2.cnpj) AS other_entities,
       SUM(c.valor_contrato)   AS total_contracts_brl
FROM br_me_cnpj.estabelecimentos e
JOIN br_me_cnpj.socios s     ON e.cnpj_basico = s.cnpj_basico
JOIN br_me_cnpj.socios s2    ON s.cnpj_cpf_socio = s2.cnpj_cpf_socio
                             AND s2.cnpj_basico <> s.cnpj_basico
JOIN br_me_cnpj.estabelecimentos e2 ON s2.cnpj_basico = e2.cnpj_basico
JOIN br_cgu_compras_governamentais.contratos c ON e.cnpj = c.cnpj_contratado
WHERE e.sigla_uf = 'SP' AND c.ano = 2023
GROUP BY 1,2,3 HAVING other_entities > 2
ORDER BY total_contracts_brl DESC
Flag companies for manual review; route to procurement governance team.
Policy Infrastructure gap prioritization

A state health secretariat identifies municipalities with critically low hospital bed coverage to prioritize federal budget allocation.

SELECT m.nome, m.sigla_uf, pop.populacao,
       ROUND(cnes.leitos_sus * 1000.0 / NULLIF(pop.populacao,0), 2) AS leitos_por_mil,
       ideb.nota_media  AS ideb_fundamental
FROM br_bd_diretorios_brasil.municipio m
JOIN br_ibge_populacao.municipio pop  ON m.id_municipio = pop.id_municipio  AND pop.ano  = 2022
JOIN ( SELECT id_municipio, SUM(leitos) AS leitos_sus
       FROM br_ms_cnes.estabelecimento WHERE ano = 2023
       GROUP BY id_municipio ) cnes   ON m.id_municipio = cnes.id_municipio
JOIN br_inep_ideb.municipio ideb      ON m.id_municipio = ideb.id_municipio AND ideb.ano = 2021
WHERE pop.populacao > 10000
ORDER BY leitos_por_mil ASC, ideb_fundamental ASC
LIMIT 50
Top 50 ranked shortlist delivered to budget committee; top 10 flagged for emergency transfer.
Investigation Electoral spending anomalies

An investigative journalist tracks whether campaign spending correlates with post-election public contract awards for companies linked to the same candidate.

SELECT cand.nome_candidato, cand.sigla_partido,
       SUM(desp.valor_despesa)          AS campaign_spend,
       SUM(cont.valor_contrato)         AS post_election_contracts,
       COUNT(DISTINCT cont.cnpj_contratado) AS linked_companies
FROM br_tse_eleicoes.candidatos cand
JOIN br_tse_eleicoes.despesas_candidato desp ON cand.id_candidato = desp.id_candidato
JOIN br_me_cnpj.socios s     ON cand.cpf_candidato = s.cnpj_cpf_socio
JOIN br_cgu_compras_governamentais.contratos cont
     ON s.cnpj_basico = SUBSTR(cont.cnpj_contratado,1,8) AND cont.ano > cand.ano
WHERE cand.ano = 2022 AND cand.sigla_uf = 'SP'
GROUP BY 1,2 HAVING post_election_contracts > 1000000
ORDER BY post_election_contracts DESC
Shortlist of 12 candidates forwarded to editorial team with source data for verification.

Data quality & governance

Fit for operational use

Documented assumptions, sensitive identifier handling, and query safety patterns — production-grade data discipline.

Partition requirements

  • Large tables (100M+ rows) require ano, sigla_uf, or mes filter to avoid scan timeouts
  • 261 tables partitioned by ano
  • 245 tables partitioned by sigla_uf
  • Always filter before aggregating on microdata

Sensitive identifiers

  • cpf — individual tax ID (personal identifier)
  • cnpj — company tax ID (14-digit)
  • Read-only access enforced at engine level
  • No PII export endpoints
  • Credentials isolated per service

Known limitations

  • Data freshness varies: CNPJ monthly, census 2010/2022, health 12–18 month lag
  • CPF joins can produce high cardinality — validate row counts
  • Survey microdata has high null rates in optional columns
  • Monetary totals require partition filters to avoid trillion-real artifacts

Sanity protocol

  • State expected order of magnitude before querying
  • Flag any result row exceeding it
  • Verify count via two independent query paths
  • Only report numbers that pass all three checks
